This is a song I wrote but never felt comfortable playing. I came up with the motif while in Switzerland and did not develop it further until the summer before going to London. The night before my departure, I was at my friend's studio, rearranging the guitar with him.

During my time in Switzerland, I did a lot of cycling, even at night when there were no road lights. Descending at a speed of 75km/h, I looked down and witnessed the entire scene of Zurich. The darkness made me a little scared, so I always played rock music to accompany my rides. I felt like a Mongolian ranger. That's when I heard this motif playing in the back of my mind. When I was writing, I deliberately chose chords that had a distinct Mongolian sound. I felt an interesting connection to my identity.
